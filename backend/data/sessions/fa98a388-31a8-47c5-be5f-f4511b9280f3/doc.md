## Introduction

Augmented Reality (AR) has emerged as one of the most transformative technologies of the 21st century, blending the digital and physical worlds to enhance user experiences. Initially conceptualized in the 1960s, AR has evolved through various technological advancements and applications, influencing fields such as education, healthcare, entertainment, and more. This research paper explores the history of augmented reality, its key milestones, and the contributions of pivotal figures in its development.

Recent advancements in AR technology have led to its integration into everyday life, particularly through mobile devices and wearables. Companies like Apple and Google are increasingly investing in AR capabilities, with products like ARKit and ARCore allowing developers to create immersive applications that enhance user interaction. Additionally, the gaming industry continues to push the boundaries of AR with popular titles, while industries such as real estate and healthcare explore AR for virtual tours and training, respectively. This ongoing innovation reflects a growing recognition of AR's potential to transform how we interact with the world around us.

## Background

The roots of augmented reality can be traced back to early experiments in computer graphics and interactive technology. Over the decades, significant advancements laid the groundwork for modern AR systems. This section outlines the key historical milestones in the development of augmented reality, highlighting notable contributors and their contributions.
