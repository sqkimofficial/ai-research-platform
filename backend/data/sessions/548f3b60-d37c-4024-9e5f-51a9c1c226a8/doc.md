## Introduction

Augmented Reality (AR) is a transformative technology that overlays digital information onto the physical world, enhancing the user's perception and interaction with their environment. It merges real-world elements with virtual enhancements, offering a unique perspective that has applications across various fields, including education, healthcare, gaming, and commerce. This research document aims to explore the origins and evolution of augmented reality, examining key milestones and contributions that have shaped its development.

## Background

The concept of augmented reality dates back several decades, evolving from early scientific exploration to the sophisticated applications we see today. The blending of digital and physical realities has been a subject of interest for researchers and developers alike, leading to significant advancements in technology and user experience.
